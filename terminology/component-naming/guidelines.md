# Introduction

Naming is a crucial aspect of Design Systems (DS), since so much of a DS efficiency relies on having a shared language between different experties. Components names need to be short and clear: users new to Welcome need to at least have an idea of what they are for at first glance.

Usually, but not always, names will follow this rule of thumb:

- Atoms and molecules have context&hyphen;agnostic names (e.g. Button, Tabs, etc.)

- Bigger elements (Organisms, Patterns or even Sections) can be named after their function.
