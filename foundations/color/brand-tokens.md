# Brand Color Tokens

The Brands tokens are part of Welcome's semantic layer and define colors that vary from one brand to another. They are Theme&hyphen;specific brand colors that adapt between light/dark modes. They can be categorized as follows:

- **Core brand colors**: `loyalty`, `primary`, `accent`

- **Surface colors:** Various surface types with high/low contrast variants

- **Utility colors**: `danger`, `success`, `warning`, `eco`, `offer`, `family`, `link`

- **Layout colors**: `outline`, `overlay`

- **State Variants colors**: `hover`, `pressed`, `focus`
