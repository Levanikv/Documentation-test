
# Grid (Web)

---

Grid is the base frame where elements are positioned on a page. As the screen size changes, the grid adapts its parameters to help define how components behave at different breakpoints.

  
| Column 1 | Column 2 | Column 3 | Column 4 | Column 5 |  
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |  
| Breakpoint | Width (px) | Columns | Gutters (px) | Margins (px) |  
| Mobile | 320 &hyphen; 767 | 4 (stretching) | 16 | 16 |  
| Tablet | 768 &hyphen; 1023 | 12 (stretching) | 24 | 32 |  
| Desktop (Small) | 1024 &hyphen; 1279 | 12 (stretching) | 24 | 32 |  
| Desktop (Medium) | 1280 &hyphen; 1439 | 12 (stretching) | 24 | 64 |  
| Desktop (Large) | 1440 &hyphen; 1919 | 12 (centered) | 24 | >64 |  
| Desktop (Extra Large) | >1920 | 12 (centered) | 24 | >64 |
